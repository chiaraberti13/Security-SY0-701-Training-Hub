Study Guide — CompTIA Security+ SY0-701

This guide provides structured coverage of all SY0-701 objectives across 18 chapters. Each chapter includes exam-focused definitions, practical examples, comparison tables, and review highlights aligned to the official CompTIA exam blueprint.
